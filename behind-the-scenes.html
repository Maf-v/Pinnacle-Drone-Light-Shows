Our Process

Explore the fascinating process that brings our drone light shows to life, discover how we combine technology, creativity, and precision to create mesmerizing displays. Here are the key steps involved:

Concept and Design

Our designers create storyboards and digital mockups to visualize the show's sequence and effects.

Animation

We use advanced software to animate and choreograph the drones' movements, creating dynamic and intricate patterns in the sky.

Flight Path Planning

The precision artistry of setting up each drone individually, designing their dance path with meticulous detail to produce a fluid and synchronized presentation.

Location Visit

We meticulously measure the area, identifying potential obstacles, and mapping out the precise coordinates for optimal drone formations.

Precision in Setup

The integration of specialized equipment into our drone fleet is a careful procedure, with our technicians skillfully attaching components to achieve a harmonious blend of functionality and performance.

Flight Test

Crucial phase where the magic begins to take shape, as the choreography and patterns are fine-tuned, guaranteeing a mesmerizing display when the curtains rise.

Real-Time Control

Behind the scenes action during the show, with our team monitoring and adjusting the drones in real-time to deliver a spectacular performance.

Backup Drones

In live events, uncertainties can arise. Having backup drones minimizes the risk of disruptions due to unforeseen circumstances.
