DRONE LIGHT SHOW GROUNDS TEAM

Position: Drone Light Show Ground Team Member

Department: Drone Light Show Production

Location: San Francisco Bay Area

As a Drone Light Show Grounds Team member, you will play a vital role in executing captivating and flawless drone light shows. You will be responsible for setting up, maintaining, and troubleshooting the equipment and infrastructure required for the shows. Your keen attention to detail and technical expertise will ensure the smooth operation of our drone fleet during rehearsals and live performances.
  • Set up and prepare the equipment and infrastructure necessary for drone light shows, including drones, ground-based equipment, control systems, and power sources.
  • Perform routine maintenance and inspections on drones and related equipment to ensure optimal performance and functionality.
  • Troubleshoot technical issues that may arise during rehearsals or live performances and take appropriate actions to resolve them promptly.
  • Collaborate with the production team to coordinate the placement of drones and ground-based equipment, ensuring optimal visual impact and safety.
  • Receive drone operation and programming training to assist in choreographing stunning aerial displays that mesmerize audiences.
  • Execute quality control measures to ensure the flawless execution of each show, including pre-flight checks and post-show evaluations.
  • Adhere to safety protocols and guidelines to guarantee the safety of personnel, equipment, and spectators during all show aspects.
  • Collaborate with team members to efficiently set up and tear down equipment at various event locations.
  • Maintain an organized equipment inventory and ensure all necessary supplies are readily available.
  • Stay updated on the latest drone technology and industry trends to contribute to continuously improving our drone light show operations.
  • Follow company policies and procedures regarding data security, confidentiality, and operational protocols.
  • High school diploma or equivalent; additional technical certifications or training in drone operation or related fields is a plus.
  • Proven experience in operating and maintaining drones or other remote-controlled devices.
  • Technical proficiency and ability to troubleshoot technical issues with drones and related equipment.
  • Excellent attention to detail and ability to follow instructions and safety protocols.
  • Strong organizational and time management skills to ensure efficient setup and teardown of equipment.
  • Physical stamina and ability to perform physically demanding tasks, including lifting and carrying heavy equipment.
  • Effective communication and teamwork skills to collaborate with team members and other departments.
  • Flexibility to work irregular hours, including evenings, weekends, and holidays, based on event schedules.
  • Passion for technology, precision, and artistic expression in the context of drone light shows.
  • Knowledge of applicable laws, regulations, and safety standards related to drone operation and public performances.

DRONE LIGHT SHOW PILOT

Position: Drone Light Show

Pilot Department: Drone Light Show Production

Location: San Francisco Bay Area

As a Drone Light Show Pilot, you will have the exhilarating opportunity to command the skies and bring mesmerizing displays to life. Your primary responsibility will be to expertly pilot a fleet of synchronized drones, creating awe-inspiring visual spectacles that captivate audiences. With a keen eye for detail and exceptional flying skills, you will execute precise maneuvers, choreographing intricate formations and patterns in the night sky.
  • Expertly pilot a fleet of synchronized drones during rehearsals and live performances, following pre-planned flight paths and executing precise maneuvers to create stunning aerial displays.
  • Collaborate closely with the creative team to translate their vision into stunning aerial performances, incorporating their desired formations, patterns, and timing into your flight sequences.
  • Ensure the safety of the audience, crew, and drones by meticulously adhering to established safety protocols and guidelines throughout the drone light show.
  • Perform routine drone maintenance and inspections to ensure optimal performance, including pre-flight checks, battery management, and firmware updates.
  • Troubleshoot technical issues that may arise during rehearsals or live performances and take appropriate actions to resolve them promptly.
  • Continuously monitor the drones’ status and make real-time adjustments to flight plans or maneuvers as needed to maintain synchronization and visual impact.
  • Collaborate with the ground team to ensure effective communication and coordination between pilots and ground-based equipment operators.
  • Stay updated on the latest drone technology and industry trends, and provide insights and recommendations for enhancing the quality and innovation of our drone light shows.
  • Contribute to developing and improving flight choreography and sequences, offering creative input and exploring new possibilities for aerial performances.
  • Maintain accurate flight logs and documentation, including flight times, maintenance records, and incident reports.
  • Assist in training and mentoring new drone pilots, sharing your expertise and best practices for safe and successful drone operations.
  • Proven drone pilot experience, exceptional flying skills, and a strong understanding of drone technology, navigation, and flight dynamics.
  • Excellent hand-eye coordination and spatial awareness allow precise control and execution of maneuvers.
  • Have a deep passion for aviation, drones, and aerial entertainment, with a keen interest in pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the field.
  • Familiarity with software and hardware used in drone operations, including flight control systems, GPS navigation, and payload management.
  • Knowledge of applicable laws, regulations, and safety standards related to drone operation and public performances.
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to execute complex flight sequences accurately and flawlessly.
  • Adaptability to changing circumstances and ability to make real-time decisions while maintaining composure and professionalism.
  • Creative mindset and ability to collaborate effectively with the innovative team to bring their vision to life through aerial performances.
  • Strong communication and teamwork skills to coordinate with the ground team, creative team, and other pilots.
  • Flexibility to work irregular hours, including evenings, weekends, and holidays, based on event schedules.
  • Physical fitness and stamina to handle the physical demands of operating drones outdoors for extended periods.

If you are ready to push the boundaries of aerial entertainment and leave audiences in awe, join us as a Drone Light Show Pilot and be part of our groundbreaking team.
